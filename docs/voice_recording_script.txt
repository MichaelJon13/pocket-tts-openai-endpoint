# Voice Recording Script (30 seconds)

Read the following text clearly and at a natural pace:

---

Hello! My name is [your name]. I'm recording this voice sample to create a custom text-to-speech voice. 

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. All your base are belong to us. She sells seashells by the seashore. Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers.

I love using text-to-speech technology. It's amazing how computers can now generate natural-sounding human speech. The future of voice synthesis is incredibly exciting.

---

**Recording tips:**
- Find a quiet room with minimal background noise
- Speak at a consistent volume and pace
- Keep a distance of about 6 inches from the microphone
- Avoid reverb and echo

**Duration:** ~30 seconds when read naturally
